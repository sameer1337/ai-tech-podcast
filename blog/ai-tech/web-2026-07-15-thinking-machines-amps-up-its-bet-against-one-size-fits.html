Google's Clean Power Project

Google's latest clean power project is making headlines, with the company investing in a major solar and battery project. Google's commitment to clean energy is nothing new, with the company having invested heavily in renewable energy sources in recent years. This latest project is just the latest example of the company's dedication to reducing its carbon footprint and promoting sustainable energy solutions.

The acquisition of Shaped is a significant development for Whatnot, as the company looks to harness the power of AI-powered recommendations. With Shaped's technology, Whatnot will be able to provide real-time recommendations and search results, helping users to discover new products and brands.

OpenAI Launches Hardware for Codex

OpenAI has launched a new hardware product, designed to be used with its Codex coding platform. As reported by The Verge, the device, called Codex Micro, is a square-shaped block of buttons that provides a more intuitive and interactive way for users to interact with the Codex platform.

The launch of Codex Micro is a significant development for OpenAI, as the company looks to provide a more comprehensive and user-friendly experience for developers. With the help of the device, users will be able to interact with the Codex platform in a more intuitive and interactive way, providing a more seamless and efficient coding experience.
